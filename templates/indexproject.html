PPE Safety Kit Object Detection

Overview

Objective: Detect PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) using YOLO (You Only Look Once) object detection algorithm. The system identifies various items crucial for safety in industrial environments.

Detected Items:
  • Hardhat
  • Mask
  • No-Hardhat
  • No-Mask
  • No-Safety Vest
  • Person
  • Safety Cone
  • Safety Vest
  • Machinery
  • Vehicle

Use Case: Designed for workplaces requiring consistent monitoring to ensure the safety of employees and reduce hazards effectively.

Dataset

Source: Roboflow's PPE Detection dataset.

Dataset Details:
  • Training Images: 2.6k
  • Validation Images: 114
  • Testing Images: 82
  • Image Dimensions: 640x640

This curated dataset ensures high accuracy in detecting PPE equipment across varied environments, supporting the system's effectiveness in real-world applications.

Key Features

  • Real-time Detection: Detect PPE kits in real-time with high precision using live video feeds or images.
  • Scalability: Capable of handling large-scale deployments in multiple zones.
  • Robust Performance: Optimized to handle different lighting and environmental conditions.

How the System Works

  1. Input: Live video or images from the camera feed.
  2. Detection: YOLOv8 processes the frames and detects PPE items.
  3. Output: The system outputs analysis results, marking violations.
  4. Alert System: Triggers alerts when PPE violations are detected.

Benefits

  • Enhanced Safety: Ensures workers are equipped with essential protective gear at all times.
  • Reduced Hazards: Minimizes workplace accidents by enforcing safety standards.
  • Efficient Monitoring: Automated system that reduces the need for manual inspection.
  • Cost-effective: Saves time and money by preventing accidents and ensuring regulatory compliance.
